Fast food, timeless flavor

We're that neighborhood spot you always come back to. The smell of stew, a table full of friends, and ice-cold beer define us. We've been cooking with the same love for years, using recipes passed down through generations. There are no secrets here, just good food, good people, and the desire to come back for more.

Good food, good mood.

Beyond our celebrated classics, our menu is a love letter to the Gulf South, changing with the seasons to bring you the freshest local catches and produce. Imagine starting your evening with plump, fried green tomatoes topped with tangy remoulade, followed by our blackened redfish, seared to perfection and served with a zesty crawfish cream sauce. And no meal is complete without a taste of our signature bread pudding, drizzled with a rich whiskey sauce that will transport you straight to the French Quarter. Whether you're joining us for a lively family dinner or a romantic night out, let our eclectic soundtrack of jazz and zydeco set the mood for a dining experience that's as lively and soulful as the city itself.
